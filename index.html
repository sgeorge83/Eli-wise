About ELI-WISE

Your AI Bible Assistant

What is ELI-WISE?

ELI-WISE is a 100% Bible-based AI assistant created to help you explore Scripture and Christian theology with clarity. It is built for believers, students, and seekers who want faithful answers — not general chat, opinions, or worldly advice.

Ask about Bible passages, doctrine, prayer, salvation, the character of God, Christian living, and related theological topics. Questions outside Scripture and theology are respectfully declined.

Knowledge base

Every answer is grounded in a curated library of authentic, public-domain Christian sources — not random internet content. Retrieved passages are cited so you can see where the answer comes from.

How answers are formed

ELI-WISE uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). When you ask a question, the system first searches the knowledge base for relevant Scripture and theology, then forms a response based on those sources — with citations shown beneath each reply.

This approach is designed to reduce hallucination and keep answers tied to real biblical and theological material, rather than inventing verses or doctrines.

What to expect

  • Clear, focused questions about the Bible and theology receive the best answers.
  • Sources are always shown so you can read and verify the material yourself.
  • Off-topic questions (weather, politics, coding, general advice, etc.) will not be answered.
  • Session limit: up to 40 questions per conversation without refreshing. Tap New Chat to continue. History is not saved after refresh.
A note on accuracy: ELI-WISE is a study aid, not a replacement for God's Word, your church, or a trained pastor. While answers are source-backed to minimise error and invention, always test what you read against Scripture itself.
